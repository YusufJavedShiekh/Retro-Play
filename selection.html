Tic Tac Toe

How to Play

• The game is played on a 3×3 grid.

• Player 1 is O and Player 2 or Computer is X.

• Players take turns placing their symbol in an empty box.

• The first to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.

• If all 9 boxes fill without a winner, the game ends in a draw.

Note: Some Rules do not apply on impossible level to increase the difficulty of the game
